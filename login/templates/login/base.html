Welcome To Virtual Stock Market


The rules of the game are as follows:

  1. This game has 15 rounds. In each round you can buy or sell shares of different companies by clicking on their charts.
  2. Initially you are given with some amount of virtual currency.
  3. By clicking on company's name you see the details of the companies anytime.
  4. In each round you will have 5 minutes (except Round 0 which is of 40 minutes so that you can read the details of the companies) to buy or sell shares . After that page will refresh automatically to next round.
  5. At the end of round 15, value of your shares will be added to left money and initially provided amount will be subtracted from that. Team with the highest profit will be the winner.
  6. Stock prices will refresh in every round.
  7. News will refresh in rounds 1,3,5,7,9,11 and 13.
  8. Organisers reserve the rights to qualify or disqualify any team in any situation.
