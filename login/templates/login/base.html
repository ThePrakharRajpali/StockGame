Welcome To Virtual Stock Market


The rules of the game are as follows:

  1. This game has 7 rounds. In each round you can buy or sell shares of different companies by clicking on their charts.
  2. Initially you are given with some amount of virtual currency.
  3. In each round you will be given one minute to buy or sell shares. After that page will refresh automatically.
  4. At the end of round 7 value of your shares will be added to left money and initially provided amount will be subtracted from that. Team with the highest amount will be the winner.
  5. Organisers reserve the rights to qualify or disqualify any team in any situation.
